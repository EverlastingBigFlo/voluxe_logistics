More About Voluxe Logistics

01

Breaking The Barriers Of Transportation

Voluxe logistics offers a wide range of services aimed at breaking a major barrier of transportation by providing different means of transportation to offer swift delivery to customers.

02

Real Time Delivery

Deliveries at voluxe are carried out and monitored by experts, oversees the delivery process, right from the take off point in various stations nationwide to the point of delivery.

03

Door to Door Delivery Service

Our watch word at Voluxe Logistics is “Convenience”. We offer Door to door delivery services by making our customers get package at a convenient point.

04

Reversed Logistics

Packages sent out for delivery at Voluxe Logistics are 100% safe and secure, but if any package is been tampered with or not packaged well. It can be sent back to us at no extra cost.
